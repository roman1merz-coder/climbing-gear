Your foot profile

Foot type

Egyptian foot shape — big toe is the longest. Slightly wide forefoot with a medium-height arch. Moderate asymmetry.

Key measurements

Foot length: 261 mm · Width: 101 mm · Arch height: 32 mm · Heel width: 64 mm · Volume: medium.

What this means

Fit implications

Your Egyptian toe profile means asymmetric lasts will feel most natural. The medium arch works well with shoes that have moderate midsole stiffness. Your slightly wide forefoot may feel cramped in low-volume models — look for "wide" or standard-volume options.

Size estimate

Based on your measurements, your climbing shoe size is likely EU 40–41 depending on the brand and how aggressively you want to downsize.
