What This Means

Your Foot Shape

You have a Roman toe form with very wide forefoot and heel. Throughout a wide profile.

Beyond the obvious: Given your Roman toes, rather avoid very pointy shoes. These might squeeze your toe tips, even if the width of the shoe fits you.

What Your Current Shoe Fit Tells Us

Your Scarpa Instinct VSR (at street size) is relaxed for Scarpa; your La Sportiva Mandala (2 down) is typical for La Sportiva.

Your Scarpa Instinct VSR's empty heel is most likely a sizing issue — the relaxed downsize creates an empty heel. Your La Sportiva Mandala's empty heel is contradictory — its narrow heel cup should be too small for your wide heel. We suspect your heel does not fit inside the cup and hence leaves the empty space at the bottom which you perceive as empty feel.

Your toes feel squeezed across all your shoes. Likely causes: none of your shoes are built for Roman toes and all your shoes are narrower than your wide forefoot. For next picks we filter for Roman-compatible toe boxes and look at wider lasts.

What to Look For

Based on your scan and current shoe fit, we target shoes with Roman toe form, wide forefoot width, and wide heel width.

Given your preference for sport climbing outdoors on limestone and a moderate fit, we prioritize balanced-stiffness velcros or lace-ups with moderate downturn and moderate asymmetry.

The softer, stiffer, and budget tiers below offer alternative options around this target.
