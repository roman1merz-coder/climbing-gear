Foot Scanner

Take a photo of the sole of your foot for shape analysis.

  1. Place your phone face-up on the floor (screen pointing at the ceiling)
  2. Hold your bare foot about a hand-width above the screen
  3. Align your foot inside the dashed guide
  4. Tap the shutter button to capture
